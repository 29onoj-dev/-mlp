About

Mindustry is an open-source-ish tower-defense / factory automation hybrid. This portal offers a simple way to launch the web build in a modal iframe, links to downloads, and a tiny troubleshooting checklist if the game won't load in your browser.

How to play

  1. Click Play in browser.
  2. If the game doesn't appear, follow the troubleshooting steps below.
  3. Prefer an offline build? Use the download links in the Download section.

Troubleshooting

  • If the embed stays blank, your network may block the remote site or the site disallows iframes. Click the official web link to open the game in a new tab.
  • Consider using a trusted VPN if your network restricts gaming sites (check policies of the network owner before using).
  • For best performance, use Chrome, Edge, or Firefox and enable hardware acceleration.
